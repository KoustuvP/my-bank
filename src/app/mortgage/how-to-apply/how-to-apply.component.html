How to apply for your mortgage

You can continue and complete your mortgage application by choosing from one of the options below.

Online

Without Advice
Continue online if you're comfortable selecting your own mortgage. You'll choose and select a mortgage from our range without receiving advice from a Bank Mortgage Adviser.

What happens next?

You can continue online and complete a mortgage application straight away.

In most cases, we'll be able to give you an instant decision,subject to checking any documents we ask you to send us and a satisfactory valuation of the property.

If you're making a jo int application online, both applicants can view the information provided and documents submitted. Bank may share information about you and any information returned as part of the credit search with all applications.

By phone

With advice
Continue by phone and one of our Bank Mortgage Adviser will advioe and make a mortgage recommendation based on your circumstances.

What happens next?

We'll send you an email within one working day, which will allow you to upload documents to oonfirm your income details.

We'll then call you back, reconfirm the details you'he provided and complete the application with you by phone.In most cases we'll be able to give you an instant decision,subject to checking any documents we ask you to send us and a satisfactory valuation of the property.

You won't be able to log back into this application and continue online.
